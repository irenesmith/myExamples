Example Applications and Websites

Just a few small samples I've created for fun and learning. Some are single page applications, some are mini-websites, created for the purposes of showing a concept or design idea. If you enjoy them, share this site on social media.

API Examples

Bugify
This little utility allows you to enter the email address that you use for your Bugzilla account and retrieve a list of the issues assigned to you.

Games

Crag
Similar to Yacht, or Yachtzee, Crag involves throwing dice, three in this case, and then scoring the throw in your scoresheet. Because there are only three dice and you get only two throws, the game moves much more quickly.
Yacht
You might know this by a more familiar name. Either way, throw five dice. You'll have three attempts to achieve the best score.

JavaScript Examples

This section contains general small examples that show you how to do one generally useful thing with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Modal Dialog
There are times when you need to get information from your user before you can let them continue. Perhaps you need a user name or, in the case of a game, you might need to know how many people are playing. The example on this page shows you how to present a dialog box to the user.

Utilities

Role Playing Utilities
A few utilities for the role-playing fanatic, such as a dice throwing utilities. Roll the number and kinds of dice you wish with an optional plus or minus. For examples, 3d6+5.
Tipster
When you are at a restaurant and are sharing the bill width multiple people, this makes it easy to calculate the tip and divide it amongst the diners.
