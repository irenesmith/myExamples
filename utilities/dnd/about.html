About RPG Utilities

This mini-site was created for my own use while playing D&D. I've been playing on and off since the 90s but I only created this site recently when I picked up the dice again after a fairly long hiatus.

The tools were not created for the general public. Only a few people have permission to save characters and/or images, for example. The dice utilities are open to anybody who has the address of this website.

Code on this site was writing using jQuery and PHP for database features. Content uses HTML5, CSS3, and Bootstrap 4.1 for formatting and Bootswatch themes.

Unless otherwise indicated, the images on this site are from Pixabay, a great source of royalty-free images of all kinds. If you need images for your site, you can get great ones there. You don't have to provide any credit, but the artists and photographers certainly deserve it. If you like, you can even give them the cost of a cup of coffee to show your appreciation for their work.
